## SEO Impact Analysis

### Does Google Penalize AI-Generated Content in 2026?
**Answer: NO** - Google does NOT explicitly penalize AI content if it meets quality standards. Key points:
- Focus is on content quality, not creation method
- Must demonstrate E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust)
- Avoid keyword stuffing and misleading information
- Human editing and fact-checking recommended

### Current SEO Best Practices (2026):
1. **Quality over quantity** - Focus on user value
2. **Human oversight** - Edit and verify AI content
3. **Original insights** - Add unique business perspective
4. **Local SEO** - Emphasize Jacksonville-specific expertise
5. **User experience** - Fast loading, mobile-friendly

### Do AI Chatbots Help or Hurt SEO?
**Answer: HELP** - When implemented correctly:

**Positive SEO Impact:**

- ✅ Improve user engagement metrics
